A beginner-friendly WordPress plugin for food bloggers to create SEO-optimized recipes. Streamlines recipe creation and boosts search visibility.

== Description ==

WP Recipe Genie is a simple and beginner-friendly WordPress plugin that helps food bloggers create SEO-optimized recipes with focus keywords, related keywords, internal and external links, and images. It streamlines recipe creation while improving search engine visibility.

**Key Features:**
*   Easy recipe creation for food bloggers.
*   SEO optimization with focus and related keywords.
*   Integration of internal and external links.
*   Image embedding for visually appealing recipes.
*   Improves search engine visibility for your recipes.

== Installation ==

1.  Upload the `wp-recipe-genie` plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory via FTP, or upload the plugin zip file through the 'Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin' screen in your WordPress admin area.
2.  Activate WP Recipe Genie through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3.  Once activated, you will find "WP Recipe Genie" in your WordPress admin menu. Click on it to start creating and managing your recipes.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I create a new recipe? =

After activating the plugin, navigate to "WP Recipe Genie" in your WordPress admin sidebar. Click on "Add New Recipe" to open the recipe editor. Fill in the details like recipe title, ingredients, instructions, SEO keywords, and images.

= Can I use this plugin with any WordPress theme? =

WP Recipe Genie is designed to be compatible with most WordPress themes that follow standard coding practices. If you encounter any display issues with your specific theme, please check our support forum at [Plugin URI](https://www.nidacademy.org/wp-recipe-genie) or contact us.

= Does this plugin support structured data (Schema.org) for SEO? =

Yes, WP Recipe Genie automatically adds Recipe schema markup (JSON-LD) to your recipes, which helps search engines understand your content better and can improve your chances of getting rich snippets in search results.

= What are focus keywords and related keywords? =

Focus keywords are the primary terms you want your recipe to rank for in search engines. Related keywords are secondary terms that are semantically linked to your focus keyword, helping to broaden your SEO reach. The plugin provides fields for these to guide your content creation.
